Department of Master of Computer Applications is committed to Excellence in value-based Teaching and inculcating a sense of Pride and Confidence in our Students. The Objective is to Empower our Students with Latest Technical Knowledge and Skills by providing them with the Highly Qualified Faculty, Modern Lab Facilities and Excellent Research and Development Environment.

The motivation is not just to nurture their Technical Skills but to provide Professional grooming and Guidance so that our Students come-up as thorough Professionals having Holistic Development within them. To meet the objectives, the Department pays special emphasis on Teaching and hands-on Practical Work. The Students exhibit their Learning through their Projects Based Learning Approach.

Thanks to the efforts of Our Visionary Management, Faculties and our Students, we continue to play a Leading role in our discipline.

VISION • Towards leading the way in area of Computer Application to train and nurture the Students to become successful Technocrats thus benefiting the Society and Nation.

MISSION • To provide opportunities to Students so that they understand the Theoretical Principles and apply them. • To enable the Students to continually master the evolving Tools, Techniques, Trends in Technology and Management and effectively put them in practice in a cost-effective manner.
